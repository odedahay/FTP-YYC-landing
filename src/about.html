About Us

The Unity Community is a volunteer service organization dedicated to enriching our community.

We are a diverse group of professionals, students, and community members who believe in the power of collaboration and shared knowledge. Through our events, workshops, and networking opportunities, we create meaningful connections that help individuals and our community thrive.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive community that empowers Filipino tech professionals and enthusiasts in Calgary to thrive, built on meaningful connections, mentorship, and collaboration.

Our Vision

We envision a future that showcases and celebrates an ecosystem  of innovation, expertise, and leadership that fully acknowledges  and values the impact of Filipino tech professionals, and cultivate  the next generation as we aspire to a future integral to the growth  and success of Calgary’s technology sector.

Our Values

A Bayanihan Spirit

We embrace the spirit of bayanihan, fostering a culture of collective support, collaboration, and mutual upliftment within our community and the broader Calgary tech landscape.

Knowledge and Impact

We support excellence and continuous growth in the technology field, encouraging innovation, the sharing of expertise, and the pursuit of impactful contributions by our members.

Optimism and Belonging

We are committed to a welcoming and equitable community in which every member feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives, talents, and shared celebrations for a just future.

Our Goals

Connect and Develop

To cultivate a dynamic platform with meaningful connections through networking events, shared resources, and career development, to build a supportive community for Filipino tech professionals in Calgary.

Advocate and Innovate

To ensure the interests and contributions of the Filipino tech community are recognized and valued through strategic initiatives that showcase expertise and achievements, and create pathways to promote innovation within the Calgary tech ecosystem.

Strengthen and Empower

To foster a safe, diverse, and equitable environment that encourages unified collaboration, celebrates inclusive perspectives, and empowers collective progress, to strengthen the cultural contributions that enrich Calgary's tech community.
